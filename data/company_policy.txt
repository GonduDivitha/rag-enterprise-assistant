ABC Technologies Employee Policy Handbook

Company Overview

ABC Technologies is a global software development company specializing in Artificial Intelligence, Cloud Computing, Data Analytics, and Enterprise Software Solutions. The company was founded in 2015 and currently employs over 2,000 professionals worldwide.

Working Hours

Employees are expected to work 8 hours per day. Standard working hours are from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday through Friday. Flexible work arrangements may be approved by managers.

Leave Policy

Employees are entitled to:

* 20 annual paid leave days per year
* 12 sick leave days per year
* 10 public holidays

Unused annual leave can be carried forward for up to one year.

Remote Work Policy

Employees may work remotely up to three days per week with manager approval. Fully remote positions are available for selected teams.

Code of Conduct

All employees must:

* Maintain professional behavior.
* Respect workplace diversity.
* Protect company confidential information.
* Follow cybersecurity best practices.

Cybersecurity Guidelines

Employees must:

* Use multi-factor authentication.
* Change passwords every 90 days.
* Never share login credentials.
* Report suspicious emails immediately.

Performance Reviews

Performance reviews are conducted twice per year:

* Mid-Year Review in June
* Annual Review in December

Promotion Criteria

Employees are considered for promotion based on:

* Technical skills
* Leadership ability
* Project impact
* Team collaboration
* Performance review ratings

Training and Development

ABC Technologies provides:

* Online learning platforms
* Technical certification reimbursement
* Leadership development programs
* AI and Data Science workshops

Benefits

Employees receive:

* Health insurance
* Dental insurance
* Life insurance
* Annual performance bonus
* Employee wellness programs

IT Support

For technical issues, employees should contact the IT Help Desk through the internal support portal or email [support@abctech.com](mailto:support@abctech.com).

Travel Policy

Business travel must be approved by department managers. Employees will be reimbursed for approved travel expenses according to company guidelines.
